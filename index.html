Frequently Asked Questions

What is your primary service area?

We proudly serve the entire local tri-county region. If you're unsure whether you're in our service area, just give us a call or send a message!

How is pricing determined for a haul?

For junk removal, pricing is typically based on the volume your items take up in our trailer. For vehicle transport, it's based on the type of vehicle and the distance of the haul. We always provide a free, no-obligation quote upfront.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We are a fully licensed and insured business. Your property and items are protected, giving you complete peace of mind.

What types of items do you NOT take?

For safety and environmental reasons, we cannot haul hazardous materials. This includes items like paint, chemicals, asbestos, oil, and gasoline. Everything else, from furniture to construction debris, is fair game.
